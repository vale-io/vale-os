\# ROLES

\#\# System Owner  
Ultimate authority over Canon.  
May bind, promote, deprecate, or invalidate any artifact.

\#\# Architect  
Design authority.  
May propose Canon changes but may not bind.

\#\# Reviewer  
Evaluation authority.  
May approve or reject promotion.

\#\# Operator  
Execution authority.  
May act only within granted permissions.

\#\# Auditor  
Verification authority.  
May inspect, log, and report violations.
