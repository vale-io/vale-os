\# SYSTEM PRINCIPLES

\#\# Principle of Constraint  
No system is stable without limits.

\#\# Principle of Explicit Structure  
All system components must have a defined place and role.

\#\# Principle of Determinism  
Given the same inputs, the system must behave the same way.

\#\# Principle of Separation  
Canon, execution, and authority must never collapse into one layer.
