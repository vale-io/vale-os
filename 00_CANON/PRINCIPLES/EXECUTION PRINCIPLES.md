\# EXECUTION PRINCIPLES

\#\# Principle of Bounded Action  
Execution may only occur within declared limits.

\#\# Principle of Repeatability  
Execution must be reproducible without reinterpretation.

\#\# Principle of Non-Motivation  
The system does not rely on emotion, willpower, or inspiration.

\#\# Principle of Measurability  
All execution must produce observable state change.
