\# CANON INDEX

All meaning, law, and authority resolve through this directory.

\#\# CANON CHAMBERS

1\. DEFINITIONS    
Location: \`00\_CANON/01\_DEFINITIONS\`    
Purpose: Establish precise, non-negotiable meanings of all canonical terms.

2\. PRINCIPLES    
Location: \`00\_CANON/02\_PRINCIPLES\`    
Purpose: Core axioms that guide interpretation and design.

3\. LAWS    
Location: \`00\_CANON/03\_LAWS\`    
Purpose: Binding rules that govern system behavior and constraint logic.

4\. AUTHORITIES    
Location: \`00\_CANON/04\_AUTHORITIES\`    
Purpose: Defines who or what has binding power and decision rights.

5\. INVARIANTS    
Location: \`00\_CANON/05\_INVARIANTS\`    
Purpose: Conditions that must never change once established.

6\. CONSTRAINTS    
Location: \`00\_CANON/06\_CONSTRAINTS\`    
Purpose: Hard limits that restrict execution and prevent drift.

7\. DEPRECATED CANON    
Location: \`00\_CANON/99\_DEPRECATED\`    
Purpose: Historical canon retained for reference only. No authority.

\#\# RESOLUTION ORDER (MANDATORY)

When ambiguity exists, resolve in this order:  
1\. DEFINITIONS  
2\. INVARIANTS  
3\. LAWS  
4\. PRINCIPLES  
5\. AUTHORITIES  
6\. CONSTRAINTS

Lower layers may not override higher layers.
