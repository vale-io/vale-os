\# SYSTEM TERMS

\#\# Canon  
The authoritative source of meaning, law, and constraint.

\#\# Runtime  
The operational environment where execution occurs.

\#\# Authority  
An entity with the right to bind, promote, or invalidate artifacts.

\#\# Audit  
A formal review to verify compliance with Canon.

\#\# Promotion  
The act of advancing an artifact to a higher authority state.
