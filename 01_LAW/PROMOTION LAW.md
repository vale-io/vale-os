\# PROMOTION LAW

\#\# Law of Readiness  
Artifacts advance only when criteria are met.

Violation → Promotion denied.

\#\# Law of Review  
All promotions require review confirmation.

Violation → Promotion suspended.

\#\# Law of Finality  
Promoted artifacts cannot regress.

Violation → Artifact deprecated.

\#\# Law of Trace  
All promotions must be logged.

Violation → Promotion invalid.
