\# ARTIFACT BINDING LAW

\#\# Law of Immutability  
Bound artifacts may not be modified.

Violation → Artifact deprecated.

\#\# Law of Identity  
Each artifact requires a unique ID.

Violation → Binding denied.

\#\# Law of Canon Entry  
Only reviewed artifacts may bind to Canon.

Violation → Artifact remains unbound.

\#\# Law of Record  
All bindings require an ARTIFACT\_RECORD.

Violation → Binding blocked.
