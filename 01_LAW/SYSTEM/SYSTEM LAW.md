\# SYSTEM LAW

\#\# Law of Stability  
A system state is valid only if all required Canon layers exist.

Violation → System enters INVALID state.

\#\# Law of Explicit State  
The system must always declare its current state.

Violation → Execution is blocked.

\#\# Law of Isolation  
A failure in one domain may not alter another domain.

Violation → Affected artifact is quarantined.

\#\# Law of Recovery  
Invalid states must resolve through defined lifecycle paths.

Violation → Manual authority intervention required.
