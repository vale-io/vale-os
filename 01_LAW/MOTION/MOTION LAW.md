\# MOTION LAW

\#\# Law of Authorized Motion  
Movement may occur only when explicitly permitted.

Violation → Motion denied.

\#\# Law of Directionality  
Artifacts may only move forward through lifecycle stages.

Violation → Reverse motion blocked.

\#\# Law of Singular Motion  
An artifact may not exist in multiple lifecycle states.

Violation → Artifact invalidated.

\#\# Law of Completion  
Incomplete artifacts cannot advance.

Violation → Promotion denied.
