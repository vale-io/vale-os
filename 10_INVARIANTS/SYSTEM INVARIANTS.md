\# SYSTEM INVARIANTS

\#\# Invariant of Canon Supremacy  
Canon always outranks execution, tooling, and convenience.

\#\# Invariant of Determinism  
Identical inputs must produce identical outcomes.

\#\# Invariant of Explicit State  
The system must always declare its state.

\#\# Invariant of Layer Separation  
Canon, Law, Authority, Execution, and Work must remain separate.
