\# AUTHORITY INVARIANTS

\#\# Invariant of Explicit Authority  
No action without explicit authority.

\#\# Invariant of Separation of Duties  
Creation, review, and binding may not be performed by one role.

\#\# Invariant of Auditability  
All authority actions must be logged.

\#\# Invariant of Revocation  
Authority may be revoked if invariants are violated.
