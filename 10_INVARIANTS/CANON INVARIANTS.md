\# CANON INVARIANTS

\#\# Invariant of Immutability  
Bound Canon entries cannot be modified.

\#\# Invariant of Reference Integrity  
Canon references must always resolve to existing nodes.

\#\# Invariant of Singular Truth  
Canon may not contain conflicting definitions.

\#\# Invariant of Traceability  
All Canon changes must be traceable to authority.
