\# ARTIFACT INVARIANTS

\#\# Invariant of Identity  
Every artifact has a unique ID.

\#\# Invariant of Lifecycle Singularity  
An artifact may exist in only one lifecycle state.

\#\# Invariant of Record  
Every artifact must have an ARTIFACT\_RECORD.

\#\# Invariant of Binding Finality  
Once bound, an artifact cannot re-enter work states.
